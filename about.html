We started this business in 2017 by selling cheese popia, overtime our business gain more popularity especially with our customer who likes cheesey popia
First we started off with just a small basic cheese popia, now currently in just 6 years in business we have released about 2 new flavour, which is cheesey
spicy popia and plain crispy popia, the reason on why we rarely release new flavour is that we want to keep the authenticity and original root of our product
especially our customer main reason as to why they buy it is that they want to eat our cheese popia.


Our business also uses natural ingredients that do not contain preservatives in order to maintain quality and our customer satisfaction, not only that we also
prioritise our customer satisfaction, thus it is why all of our products is sealed properly in order to maintain quality. Our factory is in Kajang.
